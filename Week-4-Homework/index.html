Top Ten Anime Fights of All Time!


This is a list of the top ten anime battles of all time in my opinion, if you feel differently it's probably because you're wrong.



  1. 10/10 Gon vs. Pitou "Hunter x Hunter"


    Gon is upset that the cat-ant killed one of his friends and used the friends dead body as a puppet to train her soldiers, this causes Gon to age by 25 years gaining all of the skills he would have gained over that time and opens a can of whoop ass.


  2. 9/10 Luffy vs. Katakuri "One Piece"


    Katakuri is literally better than Luffy in every way, he is superior on all fronts, but some how Luffy come's out on top


  3. 8/10 Netero vs. Meruem "Hunter x Hunter"


    Netero, the chairman of the Hunter Association, takes on Meruem the King of the Chimera Ants, the fight is of such epic proportions Netero loses two limbs and creates a hole in the earth where the rest of the battle takes place.


  4. 7/10 Midoriya vs. Bakugou "My Hero Academia"


    Midoriya, after a lifetime of taking the brute of Bakugou's bullying, has had enough and is ready to show Bakugou what he is actually made of. This clash of childhoods has to be intervened by All Might himself before the young lads do irreversible damage.


  5. 6/10 Levi vs. Beast Titan "Attack on Titan"


    Everyone knows that Levi is arguably the most powerful human in the anime, besides maybe Mikasa. So to see him in action at his full capacity was a sight to behold.


  6. 5/10 Naruto vs. Sasuke "Naruto Shippuden"


    Naruto and Sasuke have a clash of beliefs, each racing to become the next Hokage. Sasuke want's to embody hate and take on the burden of shouldering that hate from the rest of the world, he see's killing Naruto as the only way to guarantee a future lacking the darkness the current world faces.


  7. 4/10 Gohan vs. Cell "Dragon Ball Z"


    Cell tells Gohan not to get cocky, and asks the young Saiyan if he really thinks he can beat him. Gohan responds by asking Cell what he thinks. Cell is shocked and calls Gohan arrogant. He tells Gohan that he will show him his true power, and proceeds to power up, knocking everyone but himself and Gohan away.


  8. 3/10 Jotaro vs. Dio "JoJo's Bizarre"


    Jotaro approaches DIO openly with the intent of beating him to a bloody pulp. Amused to see such recklessness, DIO decides to play with Jotaro and enters in a contest of strength between Star Platinum and The World, the two Stands begin to clash, demonstrating that they are physically even when they exchange flurries of punches.


  9. 2/10 All Might vs All for One "My Hero Academia"


    All Might attacks All For One and the villain catches both of his fists in mid-air. He's able to hold All Might back and their confrontation creates a large shockwave. Both of them recover as they taunt one another about their new weaknesses.


  10. 1/10 Rock Lee vs Gaara "Naruto Shippuden"


    Guy tells Rock Lee that it is okay to remove the training weights from his ankles in preparation for his fight with Gaara, the other ninjas scoff at the asinine possibility that simple training weights could make up the difference in skill... until the weights hit the ground with the force of 20k pounds each.
